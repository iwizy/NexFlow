# Code of Conduct

NexFlow aims to be a practical, respectful, and technically serious open-source community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different backgrounds, skill levels, and use cases.
- Critique ideas and designs, not people.
- Assume good intent, then clarify when something is ambiguous.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the specification and ecosystem.
- Make room for hobby, research, startup, enterprise, and open-source perspectives.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or reviews.
- Bad-faith security, licensing, or governance arguments.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate discussions, close issues, request edits, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project.

Reports can be sent through the contact path in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) when privacy is needed.

## Maintainer Standard

Maintainers are expected to apply this code consistently, explain decisions when practical, and keep community trust ahead of personal preference.
